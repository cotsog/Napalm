Requirements:

  • Xcode 8.0 or later

  • Swift 3.0 or later

Installation:

Although Napalm is not yet availibale through Cocoapods, you can get it from Github, like this:

target '<Target-Name>' do
    pod "Napalm", :git => 'https://github.com/Napalm-Framework/Napalm.git'
end
